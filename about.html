Jobe Shores

Folk and Americana songwriter from Chandlerville, IL.

Upcoming Shows Press Website

Jobe Shores is a folk songwriter born and raised in Chandlerville, IL, writing and performing for nearly twenty years in the tradition of John Prine and Todd Snider. His songs are about things that actually happened: people he knows, places that exist, moments that don't make the news but probably should.

Songs rooted in real places and real people. Midwest Americana with nothing to prove.
